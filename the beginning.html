the beginning

  • sigh

    i look at you and i sigh,

    i wonder why

    you aren’t mine.

    wouldn’t it be sweet

    if we could meet

    everyday?

    wouldn’t it be fun

    to have someone

    to love you?

  • confess

    i want to have lunch with him

    and ask him questions

    that is such a silly suggestion!

    stop thinking about it

    he won’t say yes.

    you’re starting to like him

    won’t you confess?

  • wonderland

    with flushed cheeks and a heart full of love

    i drifted into a wonderland

    you were there

    with me

    we were walking by the sea

    hand in hand

    isn’t it lovely

    to be in love and be so free?

    but will it be

    that you and me

    be together in reality?

  • maybe

    it isn’t easy

    to bottle up my feelings

    i’m thinking about you

    while staring at the ceiling

    can it not be true

    that me and you

    be called an “us”?

    can it not be true

    that our “maybes” one day

    shall become a “must”?

  • dreaming pt. 1

    your serene face

    comes every day

    in my dreams

    your devilish smile

    sends shivers of delight

    every time i see it

    those “rosy” lips

    chant promises to me

    that you can’t keep

    please come everyday,

    i say.

    you smile

    for a while

    until you vanish again.

    and slowly,

    very slowly,

    my eyes open

    and i am awakened from this lovely dream.

  • dreaming pt. 2

    let us hold our hands again

    like we did in my dream

    let’s laugh our hearts out

    and play stupid games

    do you know how happy we were

    in my dream of me and you?

    the reality is so different

    where you don’t even have a clue!

  • transitions

    a reply from you

    changed everything I knew

    a walk in the dark

    brought us closer than apart

    the night we sat at your window, wondering, confused

    are we meant to be or is it just a ruse

    we kiss and cuddle and

    suddenly it’s day

    now’s the time to run away

  • run

    i thought you would run away

    didn’t know you intend to stay

    now i wait every thursday

    to kiss your beautiful face

  • how to begin?

    can i tell you something?

    the big things and the little things?

    i ate an apple today.

    came back from work too late.

    i shampooed my hair twice.

    bought strawberries for half the price!

    oh and i also took a walk;

    what a bummer that we couldn’t talk!

    can i tell you the little things?

    will you want to listen?

    can i show you all my pictures

    and show the brownian motion

    of the thoughts inside my head?

    or should i keep it all to myself instead?

    i don’t know how to be close to you.

  • a rainy day

    we were walking together

    talking about the weather

    the sun looked beautiful

    the breeze was soft and cool.

    suddenly you feel a drop

    on your feet and then on top

    of your head, your hand, your lips;

    you joked how it was a perfect moment to kiss

    i laughed, told you “let’s run!”

    the nearest shed was the deserted station.

    we sat at a bench, you were smiling looking at me.

    “what?” i ask,

    “you’re so pretty”, you said

    and smiled again

    there was silence between us, except for the rain…

    “i wasn’t joking earlier, you know”,

    i hear you say after a minute or so.

    it was still raining,

    you were so close to me.

    i gave up resisting and let us be…

    the rain stopped soon, the sun set down.

    we were walking again,

    your hand around me, a smile on my face

    we weren’t taling

    you weren’t joking

    it was a perfect rainy day with you

  • lovely

    my oh my oh my

    look at your beautiful smile

    look how it fills me with joy,

    warmth and so much love

    my eyes

    are wide open

    to soak all of you forever in my mind

    we are in shortage of time

    but so many stories still left to tell

    would you share yours first

    or should i tell you mine?

    is this fine?

    for us to be who we were are?

    or would you like to cross the line?

  • in love again

    i am in love again.

    this time it is really different

    he listens to me like no one ever did before

    and remembers the most trivial informations

    about me.

    i am in love again.

    and this time, i am telling the whole world

    (except for my parents, of course!)

    but i think they will know soon too

    because i’ve fallen real bad for him

    and i don’t know how to get back up.

    i am in love again.

    i don’t know if it is a dream

    but damn he makes me feel so seen!

    i want to love him forever,

    it’s the happiest i have ever been!

  • sharing pasts

    thank you for baring your heart out to me

    you didn’t really have to,

    yet you did

    my heart feels for you deeply —

    for all the moments of pain and heartaches,

    failed expectations, betrayals, those long breaks

    of silences and those moments when you’d take

    to look inside of you.

    thank you for baring your heart out to me

    for showing this little girl

    what love can truly be

    and that a man can be vulnerable too

    that you have it in you

    to love and be loved back unconditionally.

  • art of love

    loving is an art

    but you do not have to perfect it

    loving is addictive

    you can never not want it

    so when she says “you really love being loved”

    would she not have loved it too?

    i know i do

    i know you do too

    wouldn’t the world be a better place

    if only we could love a little more?

  • a girl in love

    I don’t wish to look at my phone
    constantly

    to see

    what’s up with you


    who you’re with


    what you’re thinking

    but i also wish to know all of it

    every

    single

    second

    what’s a girl in love to do?

  • first date

    a vivid hue i saw outside

    as we wandered through the park,

    hands entwined

    savoring stolen glances

    and whispered secrets.

    each touch, a brushtroke on the canvas

    the canvas of our day was quite a sight!

    every stolen kiss was a silent vow

    to love each other a little more

    the only witness was the squirrel that stood by…

    our sweet love surpassed the sugar high

    from the slushies we had;

    we reveled in our clandestine dance

    isn’t it lovely to give love a chance?
